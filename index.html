The Materials

We source the finest precious metals and gemstones to ensure every piece is a legacy.

Pure & Solid Gold

Specializing in 18K and 22K yellow, white, and rose gold. Our gold works are hand-finished to a mirror shine, providing a rich, warm glow that only high-purity metal can offer.

Brilliant Diamonds

From GIA-certified solitaires to intricate pavé designs, our diamonds are selected for superior fire and brilliance. We focus on ethical sourcing and precision setting.

Premium Silver

Our sterling silver collection is designed for the modern lifestyle, featuring rhodium plating to prevent tarnishing and ensure a long-lasting, brilliant white finish.

Our Expertise

Professional care and bespoke craftsmanship for your most cherished items.

Watch Repair

Expert servicing for luxury and mechanical timepieces. From movement overhauls to crystal replacement and water-resistance testing.

Battery Replacement

On-site high-quality battery installation while you wait. We service all major brands ensuring proper seals are maintained.

Jewelry Repair

Master goldsmithing services including chain soldering, prong rebuilding, and expert stone resetting to keep your jewelry secure.

Jewelry Cleaning

Professional ultrasonic and steam cleaning treatments that restore the original fire and sparkle to your gemstones and metals.

Custom Jewelry

From sketch to reality. We offer bespoke 3D modeling and hand-casting services to create a piece that is uniquely yours.

Engraving

Personalize your treasures with precision laser engraving. Ideal for wedding bands, timepieces, and commemorative gifts.
